Take a Journey Through Time

Greece is the birthplace of an inspirational culture that has helped shape the modern world. On your whirlwind tour of the ancient cities in modern Greece, you'll experience first-hand the things that make Greece a unique, inspirational culture:

History

Culture

What to Expect

Spend 10 days discovering and connecting with all that came before. You'll be brought on a personal tour of the major regions of Greece to see how it shaped empires that shaped it.

See the Itinerary
Krete
See former Minoan empire - the oldest civilization in Greece and the home of King Midas and the Minotaur
Sparta
Visit the land of the famous 300 spartans that kept the Persians at bay before Greece was one country.
Athens
See the birthplace of democracy, theatre, and the crown jewels of classical Greece.
Macedonia
Visit the birthplace and empire of Alexander the Great - the first person to conquer Europe and Asia, and Caesar's inspiration for the Roman Empire.
Ithaca
Visit the home of Odysseus, who spent 10 years returning home in his epic journey back from the Trojan war.
Mykonos
Blow off some steam in beautiful Mykonos, an island known for its free expression.
